Skills

▎TECHNICAL SKILLS

MACHINE DESIGN: Skills in designing mechanical components and systems, including gears, bearings, and linkages.

CAD SOFTWARE PROFICIENCY: Familiarity with computer-aided design (CAD) software like SolidWorks, AutoCAD, or CATIA for creating 2D and 3D models.

MANUFACTURING PROCESSES: Knowledge of various manufacturing techniques such as machining, welding, casting, and additive manufacturing.

CONTROL SYSTEMS: Understanding the principles of control theory and how to apply them to mechanical systems.

* FINITE ELEMENT ANALYSIS (FEA): Skills in using FEA software to simulate physical phenomena and analyze structural integrity.

PROGRAMMING SKILLS: Basic knowledge of programming languages (e.g., MATLAB, Python) for simulations and data analysis.

▎SOFT SKILL

COMMUNICATION: Ability to clearly convey ideas and technical information through
written reports and presentations.

TEAMWORK: Collaborating effectively with peers on projects and in group settings.

TIME MANAGEMENT: Prioritizing tasks and managing time effectively to meet deadlines.

ADAPTABILITY: Being open to new ideas and technologies in a rapidly evolving field.

ATTENTION TO DETAIL: Ensuring accuracy in designs, calculations, and documentation.

▎ANALYTICAL SKILLS

PROBLEM-SOLVING: Ability to identify problems, analyze them, and develop effective solutions

CRITICAL THINKING: Evaluating information logically to make sound engineering decisions

DATA ANALYSIS: Skills in interpreting data from experiments or simulations to draw conclusions.

▎PROJECT MANAGEMENT SKILLS

PROJECT PLANNING: Ability to plan and organize projects from conception to completion.

BUDGETING: Understanding cost estimation and resource allocation for engineering projects.

RISK MANAGEMENT: Identifying potential risks in projects and developing mitigation strategies.

▎PRACTICLE SKILLS

HANDS-ON EXPERIENCE: Familiarity with laboratory equipment, tools, and machinery used in mechanical engineering.

PROTOTYPING: Skills in building prototypes to test concepts and designs.

TESTING AND EVALUATION: Conducting experiments and tests to evaluate the performance of mechanical systems.

CURIOSITY: A desire to learn about new technologies, materials, and methods in
mechanical engineering.

▎INTERNET OF THINGS (IOT)

IOT: Network of physical objects, or "things," embedded with sensors, software, and network connectivity, enabling them to collect and share data, facilitating communication and automation.
